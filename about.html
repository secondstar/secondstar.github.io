Our Toolbox

Second Star Labs was founded as a Ruby on Rails shop, and while we have broadened our expertise significantly since then, more often than not, Rails is the right tool for the job.

Mobile.

For cases when the web just isn't enough, we love diving into the intricate and beautiful world of dedicated mobile development.

Responsive design.

The ecosystem of the Internet is changing before our eyes – flexible, retina-enabled, mobile-ready websites are here to stay, and we couldn't be more excited for the future of the web.

Any means necessary.

iOS, Android, Ember.js, Knockout.js, Backbone, Go, Elixir, Sinatra ... the list goes on. We pride ourselves in finding the right solution for the problem at hand.

Our Services

Launch preparation.

For clients who just want to test out an idea, we're happy to host a full wireframing & story carding session, followed by initial design, branding and proof-of-concept prototypes. We'll help you lay the groundwork necessary to get your project started in the right direction.

Training.

We firmly believe that the best way to learn is to pair. We offer flat rates for small team training, either at our offices or yours. We'll provide instruction, one-on-one pairing sessions, and talks focused on specific topics relevant to your team & project.

Consulting.

Working with Hashrocket goes beyond code & pixels – we help our clients with their team & process as well as their project. We offer comprehensive code, design and process reviews, and can help you with everything from your data infrastructure to your interviewing & hiring process.

Project development.

And, of course, we'll help you take your project from dream to reality. We've designed & built everything from highly technical internal tools to customer-facing e-commerce and marketing sites. Whether you're looking to launch something brand new or rework a legacy project, we're ready to make it happen.
