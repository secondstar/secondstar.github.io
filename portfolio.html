  • WDW Hub Homepage

    Project description

    Second Star Labs is helping WDW Hub launch its travel site with crowd-source content. Millions of people have traveled to Walt Disney World, and some have become year-round fans. Second Star Labs helps WDW Hub connect fans and the casual traveler with information about the fun and the food.

  • WDW Hub Food

    Project description

    Second Star Labs helped WDW Hub automatically collect reviews of more than 100 restaurants around Walt Disney World. While properly crediting the sources of the information, the food section of the site regularly and automatically reaches out to review sites and flickr.com.
